## Privacy Policy

By using our stuff, you agree to this Privacy Policy.

We get info in these ways:

*   **Info you give us:** When you reach out, make an account, or buy something, you might share your name, email, address, or payment details.
*   **Auto-collected info:** We might get your IP address, browser type, device info, and how you use our site.
*   **Cookies:** We use these to make things better. You can control cookies in your browser's settings.

## How We Use Your Info

We use your info to:

*   Give you and improve our services
*   Take care of orders and payments
*   Answer your questions
*   Send you updates or deals (if you asked for them)
*   Follow the law

## Sharing Your Info

We don't sell your personal info. We might share it with:

*   Service people who help us run things (like hosting, payments, or data stuff)
*   The law, if they ask
*   Partners if we merge or get bought

Everyone who gets your data has to keep it safe and only use it how we say.

## Keeping Data Safe

We're doing what we can to protect your data. Keep in mind, nothing online is 100% secure.

## Your Rights

You have some rights about your personal info, like:

*   Saying no to marketing

## How Long We Keep Data

We only keep your personal data as long as we need it for what we said in this policy, or how long the law says we have to.
